<span data-ttu-id="f3ea5-182">IoT 是指相互联网的物理对象。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f3ea5-182">IoT refers to physical objects that are networked together.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f3ea5-183">它们有时称为 "连接的设备" 或 "智能设备"。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f3ea5-183">They're sometimes referred to as "connected devices" or "smart devices."</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f3ea5-184">来自汽车和自动售货机机的所有内容都可以连接, 并将信息从库存发送到传感器数据, 如温度和湿度。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f3ea5-184">Everything from cars and vending machines may be connected and send information ranging from inventory to sensor data such as temperature and humidity.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f3ea5-185">在企业中, IoT 通过监视和自动化提供了业务流程改进。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f3ea5-185">In the enterprise, IoT provides business process improvements through monitoring and automation.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f3ea5-186">IoT 数据可用于在大型仓库中控制气候, 或通过供应链跟踪清单。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f3ea5-186">IoT data may be used to regulate the climate in a large warehouse or track inventory through the supply chain.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f3ea5-187">当检测到冒烟时, IoT 可能会检测到化学液体, 并致电防火部。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f3ea5-187">IoT can sense chemical spills and call the fire department when smoke is detected.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="f3ea5-188">设备和信息的巨大量通常指示事件驱动的体系结构路由和处理消息。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f3ea5-188">The sheer volume of devices and information often dictates an event-driven architecture to route and process messages.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="f3ea5-189">无服务器是一种理想的解决方案, 原因如下:</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f3ea5-189">Serverless is an ideal solution for several reasons:</span></span>

* <span data-ttu-id="f3ea5-190">随着设备和数据量的增加, 启用缩放。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f3ea5-190">Enables scale as the volume of devices and data increases.</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="f3ea5-191">支持添加新的终结点以支持新设备和传感器。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="f3ea5-191">Accommodates adding new endpoints to support new devices and sensors.</span></span>
